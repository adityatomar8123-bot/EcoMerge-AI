# Legacy Notes

## Historical framing

Earlier iterations of the repo centered on ESG report extraction, SASB-structured analysis, and document-lifecycle workflows. Those ideas remain useful as background context, but the active submission direction now prioritizes a local ERP-style ESG operations model.

## Current product stance

The repo’s documented product story is now EcoSphere, with governance, carbon operations, audits, notifications, and business reporting as the primary experience.
